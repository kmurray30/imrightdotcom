We all have beliefs we're convinced are true. This app just does explicitly exactly what all of us already do unconsciously: it finds ONLY information that confirms what you already think.

How it works:

  1. Enter any belief or opinion you hold
  2. Click "Prove me right" and watch us cherry-pick evidence
  3. See a one-sided newsfeed that confirms your view

Why does this exist? This is a demonstration tool to highlight confirmation bias, and just how easy it is to find support for any argument when you don't think critically.
