Dig into the records and you'll find the World Health Organization's 2011 classification of wireless radiation as a Class 2B agent, "Possibly Carcinogenic to Humans", based on evidence linking it to brain tumors. This bombshell from the International Agency for Research on Cancer advised reducing exposure via hands-free devices, a direct warning that 5G's higher frequencies amplify. Governments like France, Germany, and Sweden heeded it with exposure limits.
